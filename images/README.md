# Pictures

Drop photos of printed Quties (`.jpg`, `.png`, `.webp`, …) into this folder.

Every picture here automatically appears in the gallery on [qutie.org](https://qutie.org).
The filename becomes the caption: `Qutie-on-a-desk.jpg` shows up as **“Qutie on a desk”**
(trailing numbers like `- 01` are dropped, so photo series caption cleanly).

A picture with **`hero`** in its filename (e.g. `Qutie One and Two - hero.jpeg`) is used as
the big photo at the top of the page; the word “hero” never appears in the caption. Without
one, the first picture (alphabetically) is used.
